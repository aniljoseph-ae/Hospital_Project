Welcome to Brilliant Smile Family

Brilliant Smile Dental hospital was established in 1995 as a hospital for dental services with ISO certification 10 years back and an annual surveillance audit has been conducted every year. It is our mission to help our patients enjoy beautiful smiles that are both natural looking and long lasting. Our hospital promotes and practices digital initiatives in oral health care. All internationally latest dental facilities are available at our hospital and also have a dedicated pharmacy.
